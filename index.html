Our Story

A Passion for Baking

Baking is my passion, and as a home baker, I take pride in creating delicious treats using only the healthiest ingredients. Every recipe is crafted with love and care, ensuring both great taste and wholesome goodness. My goal is to bring joy through baked goods that are not only delightful but also nourishing."

What started as weekend passion projects quickly gained popularity through word of mouth, and soon Geetha was receiving orders for birthday celebrations, office gatherings, and special events.

Today, our home bakery continues to create handcrafted delicacies with the same attention to detail and love as those first batches. We are committed to using only the finest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, to ensure every bite brings joy,fresh and satisfaction. Our bakes are made exclusively with wholesome wheat flour—never all-purpose flour—and we sweeten them naturally with brown sugar, avoiding white sugar entirely. This ensures that our treats are not only delicious but also nourishing.
